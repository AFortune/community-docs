Bite! Clojure

Welcome to ClojureBridge CommunityDocs! This web site has a lot of (should be in near future) documents for the ClojureBridge workshops. The documents will be used as a supportive material. ClojureBridge has a main stream document (on going now at https://github.com/ClojureBridge/curriculum); however, only one document would be unable to fit/satisfy all attendees of the workshops. Some might be programmers of other languages, some may be designers, or others are, probably, beginners of programming. For such attendees of various backgounds, providing extra labs and explanations (by coaches) is a purpose of the documents here. So that everybody can enjoy "Bite! Clojure".

The documents assume that the attendees will try lab problems with helps of coaches. Each document has advices to the coaches also(should be there). If you will volunteer as a coach, have a look at the advices to know what the attendees should learn.

Please Fork the repo, write a document, and give us a pull request! We will merge your work, which will be a part of materials at ClojureBridge workshops. Please read How to Contribute, which guides you how to write a document.
